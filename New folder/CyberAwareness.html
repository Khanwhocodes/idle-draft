Cyber Safety Tips for Students

Essential guidelines to protect your digital footprint and stay safe online.

🔒 Password Safety

Always use strong, unique passwords that combine uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid using easily guessable information like your name or birthdate, and never reuse the same password across multiple sites.

🎣 Phishing Awareness

Be cautious of unsolicited emails or messages asking for personal information. Always verify the sender's email address and never click on suspicious links or download attachments from unknown sources.

🛡️ Safe Browsing

Ensure the websites you visit are secure by looking for "https://" and the padlock icon in the address bar. Avoid downloading pirated software or visiting untrusted sites that could infect your device with malware.

📱 Social Media Privacy

Regularly review and update your privacy settings to control who can see your posts and personal details. Be mindful of oversharing information like your real-time location or sensitive daily routines.

⚠️ OTP & Scam Awareness

Never share your One-Time Passwords (OTPs) with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank, a service provider, or tech support. Legitimate organizations will never ask for your OTP over a call or text message.
